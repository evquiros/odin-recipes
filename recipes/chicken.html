Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

A quick and healthy dish featuring chicken and vegetables stir-fried in a savory sauce, perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (sliced thin)
  • Bell peppers (sliced)
  • Broccoli florets
  • Carrots (sliced)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Olive oil or sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or skillet and saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in soy sauce, toss to combine, and serve over rice.
