Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

A simple and classic Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes for a spicy kick.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (sliced)
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© sliced garlic until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cooked spaghetti; toss to coat.
  4. Mix in chopped parsley, adjust seasoning, and serve with grated Parmesan if desired.
