Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

A refreshing Italian salad with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • Ripe tomatoes (sliced)
  • Fresh mozzarella (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Arrange slices of tomato and mozzarella alternately on a plate.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve immediately as a starter or side dish.
